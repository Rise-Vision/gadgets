Bulletin is an experimental app from Rise Vision that allows you to share and collaborate on the creation of Bulletins for use on your digital signage.

Bulletin uses your Google Account to determine who you are, and your Google Drive to store and publish Bulletins. You can share and collaborate with any other Google Account user, just as you would any other Google Drive file.

To try it out, go ahead and login with your Google Account but, please remember, this is an experimental app and we make no warranties or guarantees about its life, performance or reliability.


As always, if you have questions, concerns, suggestions, or even praise, please let us know in the community.
